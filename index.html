Biography

Ariana Grande is a singer and actress from Boca Raton, FL. She got her start on Broadway in the musical "13" in 2008. However, she rose to mainstream fame when she starred in the Nickelodeon show "Victorious" with fellow 13 star Elizabeth Gillies. She released her first single "Put Your Hearts Up" in 2011 and released her debut studio album "Yours Truly" in late 2013. Just the months afte the succesful release of her fourth album "Sweetner," the songstress is already working on her fifth album. Grande recently debuted her first single from the upcoming album "Thank u, Next" which is her first single to reach number one on the Billboard charts. .
